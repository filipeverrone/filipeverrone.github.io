Bio

Filipe Verrone de Lima is a Software Engineer and PhD researcher based in São Paulo, Brazil. He currently works at Skyone, where he builds scalable cloud-native solutions and production ML systems.

He is pursuing a PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Universidade Presbiteriana Mackenzie (UPM), focusing on medical imaging, representation learning, and quality control in healthcare AI systems. His research includes bone age assessment pipelines using hand X-ray images, image quality triage, convolutional autoencoders, and YOLO-based preprocessing.

Filipe holds an MSc in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Mackenzie Presbyterian University with a focus on AI in healthcare, and a BSc in Computer Engineering. He also holds a technical degree in Electrotechnics, providing him with a strong foundation in both software and hardware systems.
